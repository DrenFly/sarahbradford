About Me

Hello, my name is Sarah, I am a creative graphic & web designer inspired by the people around me working to make a difference in the lives of others. My goal is to help your business—whether big or small—reach the people who need to hear your message most—through purposeful visual marketing. I will work with you to bring your vision to life, creating vibrant and attention-grabbing material that will make you stand out from the background noise. Most days, you will find me sketching on my balcony in Amsterdam, with my sweet dog Cooper sleeping by my side.
